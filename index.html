PayPal Charge Calculator

This is a tool for use and developed by Etaerc however it is free and open source.

PayPal typically uses its own internal currency conversion rates when processing transactions involving different currencies. These rates can vary slightly from the publicly available rates you might find on platforms like Coinbase or other currency converters. PayPal's conversion rates are usually updated regularly and are applied when converting one currency to another during transactions. Therefore, while external converters like "pyusd" on Coinbase can provide a good estimate, the exact conversion rate used by PayPal may differ slightly due to factors such as market fluctuations and PayPal's own currency exchange policies.

please monitor for changes in PayPal's currency exchange policies here: Paypal merchant fees

currently this application uses the coinbase api for the conversion rate and assumes standard rate for commercial domestic transactions to be: 3.49% + fixed fee of 0.49USD when using PayPal Checkout and PayPal Guest Checkout.
percentage rate can be checked here: Paypal merchant fees
fixed fee can be checked here: Paypal fixed fees for PayPal Checkout
